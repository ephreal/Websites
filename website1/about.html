ABOUT US

AspenLine is a high quality, custom gift supplier to the promotional products industry. Our speciality is laser engraved wood and anodized aluminum products that include pens, desk accessories, pocket tools, key rings, picture frames, magnets, and more. Our parent company has a long history of supplying similar products to the souvenier and gift industry. AspenLine has the capacity to fully meet your needs.

AspenLine only markets to promotional product distributors and does not sell to the retail public. If you are an individual or a retail business searching for custom imprinted products, please contact your local distributor. Click here to find a distributor near you.
